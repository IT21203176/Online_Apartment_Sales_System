FACILITIES

INFINITY POOL

Perched on the 63rd floor and visually merging
with the ocean, AV’s 16m infinity pool is the highest ot
door swimming pool in any residential complex or hotel in the world.

LAUNDRY
COLLECTION

The laundry unit helps people who lead a busy
life to save their time.

RECREATION
AREA

Get into shape in AV's well equipped Gym, and
relax afterwards in modern Steam and Sauna rooms.
The Games room is equipped with a variety of active and
enjoyable indoor games. There’s a pool for everyone.
With four swimming pools located within the building, there’s
one that’s sure to suit any moment. A 25 metre lap
pool for focused swimmers, a waddle pool for tiny beginners,
and a pool and jacuzzi for the entire family.

SPA &
SALON

It makes it easy for both men and women to do
beauty work in one place. There is also a spa
for mental and physical well-being.

PHARMACY &
MINIMART

Located inside the apartment area. Pharmacy and Mini
Mart seeks to be a problem solver in the neighborhood. It
focuses on improving community health outcomes and reducing
emergency room visits by providing convenient compliance
packaging, monitoring adherence to maintenance medication in 24/7 hours.
It also aims to address the food requirements in the clients
by partnering with AV Kitchen to sell fresh produce at lowered prices.
