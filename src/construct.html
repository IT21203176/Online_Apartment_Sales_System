Arabian General Construction Company

Arabian General Construction Company has a rich legacy that spans over 85 years in the construction industry. The company’s expertise has been repeatedly showcased on projects which included major advancements in Construction technology.

Currently, as a construction giant in India and overseas Arabian General Construction Company has a diverse portfolio of civil and structural engineering masterpieces, these encompass airports, hotels, giant skyscrapers, housing complexes, townships, stadia and auditoria, factories, nuclear research establishments, nuclear waste handling establishments, scientific and research companies, water treatment plants, roads, expressways, power plants and biotech facilities.

As one of the jewels in the SP Group’s crown, it works hand-in-hand within its group of companies to be able to execute turnkey projects swiftly and efficiently. These include AGCC Fabricators, AFCONS, Forbes, Sterling and Wilson, AGCC Construction Materials Group, AGCC Real Estate and Samalpatti Power Company Private Limited. Together, this conglomerate maintains and improved upon its hallmarks of safety and quality.

Now with over 3000 dedicated and qualified engineers employed by the organization, Arabian General Construction Company is moving towards a new chapter of progress, which involves accomplishing projects that will stand as proud testimony to their times, and for posterity.
