THE TOUR

EXTERIOR

STRUCTURE

AV’s structure provides greater strength, superior load
distribution, column-free interiors, enhanced natural light, and
interior flexibility.

The Diagrid design is ideal for large buildings. The use of steel
and triangular structures makes it possible for diagonal support
beams to combine gravity with lateral support. This obviates the
need for large corner columns and provides better load
distribution.

VENTILATION
SYSTEM

AV’s structure facilitates cross-ventilation and multiple
exposure to light and air in every residential unit & across the
building. The towers are oriented to the movement of the sun
to maximize the infiltration of the light and wind whilst taking
advantage of New York's wonderful tropical climate.

BED
ROOM

AV's capacious bedrooms are equipped with
an innovative wardrobe solution from TZ Austria
(Tischlerzentrum GesmbH). These built-in wardrobes finished
in proprietary techno glass™ extend to the full height of each room,
The bedrooms are fitted with neutral-toned BOEN engineered wood
from Europe, conforming to the theme of contemporary style
envisioned by our world-class interior architects .

BATHROOM

Simple, minimalist and elegant would be the best way to describe this space.
Our bathrooms are all fitted with the designer range of
Philippe Starck sanitary-ware from Duravit – Germany
to create a modern yet a time-less space. Complementing
the designer sanitary ware are Grohe fittings also from Germany.

KITCHEN

Every apartment will be equipped with an elegantly-designed
and functional modular kitchen from SEA
Bauformat of Germany fitted with signature line Burg appliances.

DINING
ROOM

Each apartment is beautifully landscaped with plenty of
light and natural ventilation, with ample
space for the whole family to spend time together.

INTERIOR ARCHITECTURE

AV’s diagrid architecture facilitates large, floor-to-ceiling glass windows, which lets in natural light. The impressive 3.3 metre high ceiling only adds to its grandeur. And its uniquely designed cross-ventilation and multiple exposures to the elements invite only the best of nature.
